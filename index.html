Cybersecurity with Computational and Artificial Intelligence (CyComAI)

CyComAI is a research group launched in 2015 by its leader Dr.-Ing. Franklin Tchakounté, who had the concern to formalize research activities in the domain of cybersecurity. This research group aims at building security barriers at the level of users and at the level of organizations. This group makes use of computational and artificial intelligence approaches to bring robustness and scalability in the proposed systems. It includes senior researchers, PhD students and Master students from the University of Ngaoundéré in Cameroon and abroad. Research areas are (but not limited to)

    • Cybersecurity
    • E-trust and e-reputation
    • Social network protection
    • Phishing detection
    • Cryptography
    • Computational/artificial intelligence

Besides its scientific and educational activities, CyComAI provides consultancy services in Computer Science, mainly in the area of Computer Security and Networks. It takes also part in the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) activities.
